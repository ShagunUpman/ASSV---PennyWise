Features of PennyWise

Automated Expense Tracking

AI tracks and categorizes your spending in real-time without manual effort.

Smart Budget Recommendations

Custom monthly budgets generated based on your spending patterns and income.

Spending Insights & Alerts

Get notified about overspending and receive suggestions to cut costs.

Adaptive Learning

The app learns from your habits and improves its recommendations over time.

Conversational AI Assistant

Ask budgeting questions and get help through our interactive chatbot.

Goal-Based Planning

Set savings goals and track your progress with personalized planning tools.

Engaging UI & Gamification

Stay motivated with a fun, visual experience that rewards good financial habits.

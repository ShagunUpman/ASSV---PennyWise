How PennyWise Works

1. Set Your Budget

Enter your total monthly budget to begin managing your finances smarter.

2. Define Spending Categories

Break your budget into categories like Food, Travel, or Entertainment.

3. Track Your Expenses

Log your expenses and watch your limits in real-time.

4. Get Instant Warnings

Receive warnings when you exceed your set spending limits.

5. Learn and Improve

PennyWise learns from your habits and suggests smarter financial moves.
